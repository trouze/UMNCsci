MINNOVATE UMN

For the mission to cultivate student innovation.

Who we are

Bridging the gap between academia and industry

Minnovate has three main goals. First, we strive to cultivate student innovation at the University of Minnesota. Not only do we hope to innovate within the confines of our campus, but it is our hope that what you learn with us will serve you in your future endeavors, impacting the world.

Our second goal is to create a community of students, professionals, and businesses. Through challenges and cases, we look to connect students to professionals and businesses of all types, in order to bridge the gap between academics and the working world.

Finally, our biggest endeavor is to build student capabilities and provide resources for entrepreneurship. We believe that entrepreneurship can be a lonely journey. Not only do we want to connect our students with other, like minded students, but we want to provide resources to level up your do-it-yourself skills.

WE BELIEVE IN CULTIVATING STUDENT INNOVATION

Through challenging cases and innovation challenges every academic year, we look to stretch our member's aptitudes for novel solutions and breaking down complex problems to develop whole solutions.

Our events can help set you apart from your peers in the job recruiting stage of your college career, giving you something to show off. We believe that the real life experience we can offer you will level up not only your technical skills, but your soft skills too.
